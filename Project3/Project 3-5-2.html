"Today's $25 million settlement agreement is a stunning reversal by Donald Trump
and a major victory for the over 6000 victims of his fraudulent university,"
Attorney General Eric Schneiderman said in a statement.

On Twitter, the president-elect hailed the agreement
as a necessary condition for focusing on running the country,
but boasted that he could have prevailed in a court proceeding.

"The only downside to being President is that
I can't fight the long but winning legal battle
defending Trump U. Too bad!"

Upon this, one has to remark that

men ought either to be well treated or crushed,

because they can avenge themselves of lighter injuries,

of more serious ones they cannot;

therefore the injury that is to be done to a man
ought to be of such a kind

that one does not stand in fear of revenge.
